Proper Disposal & Recycling

At the end of their service life, Smartium products must be disposed of responsibly to protect the environment and conserve natural resources.

Important: Electronic equipment must not be disposed of with household waste. Take your device to designated collection points for proper recycling.

Our products contain valuable materials that can be recovered and reused. Proper recycling helps:

  • Conserve natural resources
  • Prevent soil and water contamination
  • Reduce landfill waste
  • Protect human health and environment
