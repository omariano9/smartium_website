Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common technical questions

How do I update the firmware on my KNX gateway? ‚ñº
Firmware updates can be performed via the KNX bus using ETS. Download the latest firmware from the downloads section, load it into ETS, and follow the on-screen programming instructions. Ensure the device remains powered during the update process.
What is the maximum cable length for connecting to indoor units? ‚ñº
The maximum cable length is 328 ft (100 m). For installations exceeding 33 ft (10 m), install communication and power cables separately to prevent electromagnetic interference.
Can I control multiple HVAC brands with one KNX system? ‚ñº
Yes, each brand requires its specific gateway. You can integrate multiple gateways into one KNX system, allowing unified control of different HVAC brands through your building management system.
What certifications do I need to install these products? ‚ñº
Installation should be performed by certified electricians or HVAC technicians. KNX programming requires ETS certification. We recommend KNX Partner or KNX Tutor certification for professional installations.
Is technical support available in my language? ‚ñº
We provide documentation in English, French, Italian, Spanish, German, Dutch, and Portuguese. Email support is primarily in English, but we can accommodate other languages with slightly longer response times.
